the product

Niche is a connected, affordable smart device that fits onto reusable containers and accurately tracks the exact amount of food items to avoid running out or overstocking of supplies.

+
Food safe Enclosure
+
Always Connected (Wifi)
+
Adaptable (retrofit- sticky material)
+
Water and Dust Resistant

Set it, forget it.

Place niche on the container of an item you want to track.

Connect

Press the button on the niche to connect the device to our app.

Attach

Place a niche on the lid of a container holding an item you want to track.

Personalize

Set your tracking preferences and guidelines. Get a little help from niche or fully automate.
